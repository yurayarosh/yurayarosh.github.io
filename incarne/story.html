Story—Incarne

I've always loved good things. For me, a good thing is something that comes from the heart, from the heart. After all, such things cease to be just another junk, everything is fine in them: the texture, when you hold your hand and feel the structure of the material; Design, where there is nothing superfluous and catchy; reliable accessories for centuries; and, perhaps most importantly, human labor, when a thing is created with love for one's business and thought about you, about your needs.

We fill the disembodied skin and cold accessories with the warmth of life, care for people and strive for human understanding. That's why I wanted to bring this spiritual impulse into a material form. So the spirit originated, the spirit of InCarne, and you know, I was very lucky. It was lucky that on my life's path met people who share my views, who love what they do.

We are a fairly young team, we experiment a lot, constantly develop and invent a new one. And this one goal - a good thing!

Arthur,
Founder Amily,
Designer Gosha,
Leather master

I sincerely hope that we have succeeded, that you will feel this and will be able to enjoy the long service life of what we have done for you.
